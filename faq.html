Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Do I need to use my Lichess.org username and password?

Yes! You must **register using your existing Lichess.org username and password** to verify your chess rating and personalize your experience. Without this, registration is not possible.

2. Is LichessOnline affiliated with Lichess.org?

No, **LichessOnline.blogs is an independent blog website**. We provide chess blogs, community discussions, and resources related to Lichess.org, but we are not officially affiliated with them.

3. Can I play chess games on LichessOnline?

No, LichessOnline is **not a chess-playing platform**. It is a **blog and community website** where you can read chess content, join discussions, and learn strategies.

4. What pages are restricted for non-registered users?

Only registered users can access the **team page and blog page**. If you are not registered, you won’t be able to access them.

5. How do I create an account?

Click on the Signup button and enter your **Lichess username and password** along with your email. If your username is already taken, please use your real Lichess account.

6. Is my personal information secure?

Yes! We **do not share or misuse your data**. Your information is securely stored, and we prioritize privacy.

7. How can I improve at chess using LichessOnline?

We provide **high-quality blogs, tutorials, and guides** to help you improve. Check our Chess Resources page for expert advice.

8. Can I submit my own chess blogs?

Yes! If you’re a chess enthusiast, you can share your knowledge. Contact us at our email to submit your article.

9. Is LichessOnline free?

Yes! Just like Lichess.org, LichessOnline is **completely free** to use.

10. I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

If you forget your password, it can't be recover.

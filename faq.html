Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Why should I use my Lichess username to create an account?

We require you to use your **Lichess.org username** so that we can verify your chess rating and provide you with personalized chess content.

2. Is LichessOnline affiliated with Lichess.org?

No, LichessOnline.blogs is an independent platform that shares chess blogs, resources, and community discussions related to Lichess.org.

3. How do I create an account?

Click on the Signup button and enter your **Lichess username**, email, and password. If your username is already taken, choose another.

4. Is my personal information safe?

Yes! We prioritize **security and privacy**. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties.

5. Can I delete my account?

Yes! You can delete your account anytime, and all your data will be erased from our system.

6. How can I improve at chess?

We provide **top chess blogs, tutorials, and strategy guides** to help you improve. You can also check out our Chess Resources page.

7. Do you have a mobile app?

Currently, we do not have an app, but our website is **fully mobile-friendly** for a smooth experience.

8. Can I submit my own chess blog?

Yes! If you're a chess enthusiast, you can submit your chess articles. Contact us at our email for details.

9. Is LichessOnline free to use?

Yes! Our website is **completely free**, just like Lichess.org.

10. I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

If you forget your password, click on **Forgot Password** on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to your email.
